Metal Roofing & Siding Sales

Barns Unlimited is your source for top-quality metal roofing and siding materials. We manufacture and supply premium metal panels for barns, homes, and commercial buildings.

Our Metal Products

We offer 29-gauge and 26-gauge steel panels in a variety of profiles (ribbed panels, standing seam, etc.) to suit your project. All our metal panels feature a 40-year paint warranty and are engineered to withstand harsh weather. Choose from 28 standard colors and even special crinkle finish colors for a textured matte look. Matching trim, screws, and accessories are available for a complete installation.

  • Roofing Panels (cut to custom lengths)
  • Siding Panels
  • Color-Matched Trim (ridges, corner, J-trim, etc.)
  • Fasteners and Sealants
  • Skylight Panels (polycarbonate)

Whether you're re-roofing an old barn or need metal siding for a new garage, we have the materials ready for pickup or delivery. Our metal is sourced and fabricated here in the USA, ensuring high quality and supporting local manufacturing.

Why Buy Metal from Us?

  • Cut to Length: We cut panels to the exact length you need (up to 40'). This means less waste and easier installation on your building.
  • Fast Turnaround: In most cases, we can fulfill your metal order within 1-2 days. Your project won't be delayed waiting for materials.
  • Expert Advice: Not sure what you need? Our knowledgeable staff will help you calculate the right quantities and types of panels, trim, and screws. We’ll make sure you have everything required.
  • Affordable Pricing: Because we buy steel in bulk and form panels in-house, we’re able to offer very competitive pricing. Quality comes first, but it doesn’t have to break the bank.

For contractors, we offer volume discounts. For DIY homeowners, we provide guidance every step of the way. Barns Unlimited aims to be your one-stop shop for metal roofing and siding in the Ohio, Indiana, and Michigan region, and expanding.

How to Order

Ready to get materials for your project? Here's how to order from Barns Unlimited Metal Sales:

  1. Gather your measurements (length of each roof slope or wall, number of panels, trim pieces, etc.). If you have a plan or sketch, we can assist with a takeoff.
  2. Call us at 419-657-2510 or contact us here with your requirements. We will provide a free quote and lead time.
  3. We’ll schedule your pickup or delivery. Our team can load your trailer or truck at our yard, or we can deliver to your site for an added fee.

We accept cash, check, or credit card. Payment is due at pickup/delivery. If you have any questions about our metal products, don’t hesitate to reach out. We're happy to help!
